About US

We are a uniform manufacturer with over 5+ years of uniform manufacturing unit and equipped machineries along with experienced people working in the uniform industry for more than 30 years of time who make sure that they serve you with the best quality of uniform. We are specialized in customization of uniforms and work wears. We have a team of highly professional sales and admin staff combined with skilled technical background production staff. We have 100+ employees working with us.

At Cals Uniform we produce customised uniforms as per client needs that are stylish and comfortable with which clients can showcase their brand. We ensure that uniforms manufactured by us will add value to your customer proposition and will help you to stand out in the market. We ensure to give importance to the quality of the product with which you can also build your market. Cals Uniforms is a leading supplier of uniforms across industries including Hospitality, Corporate, Healthcare, Aviation, Education, Industrial, Housekeeping, Transportation & Event Management.

We provide quality service to our clients with professionalism. Our talented and experienced designers and sales staff make sure that your ideas of brand image will reflect in the uniform that we design along with a comfortable and stylish look for your employees. We are serving individual and corporate needs with the superior quality products. We work on perfection with excellent quality products and focus on timely delivery to our customers. Our ultimate goal is to delight our customer with our professional services and retain the client with us for longer term.
